About

Santiago Bar est 2002.
The spiritual home of rock, punk, whisky blues and alternative music.


Come join us for a drink or an event in our upstairs venue. We are a friendly and welcoming bar and venue with dive vibes, but always a fully stocked fridge.
We offer a large range of spirits, shooters, ales, lagers, craft beers, wines, ciders and we even have our own Santiago Cocktail menu.

Our upstairs venue is fully equipped for all your needs, whether it be a music event, a birthday or a gathering were here for all your needs! We are an honest night out in Leeds.

Please contact santiagobarleeds@gmail.com for information on hiring our venue.
